No dream is too small for a dreamer.
SarCor is like a tree with many leaves all seasons, including Portraits, Graphic design work, Web development, design, and Animation. You smile, We smile!

The name SarCor is derived from Sakhokuhle, my second name, with a meaning: "building something beautiful". I was born and raised in Kwazulu Natal, Durban, a very diverse city full of dreamers like me. SarCor is a young man full of dreams and plans to make his dreams a reality, not only dreaming but living the dream.

We chase different dreams or goals and make decisions that are life-changing each time, but no mind grows in the dark or inside the box. Leave your box and go outside, see the world, and do something you thought you couldn't do.

When I had lost hope and was struck by depression, I looked down and saw my hands. I saw an opportunity and hidden talent, and I started drawing. Looking for things to draw online, I came across an application at NEMISA for animation students. I applied with doubts, but I got it, and there my goal changed from being an animator to being a developer. I am keen to learn and develop websites.
